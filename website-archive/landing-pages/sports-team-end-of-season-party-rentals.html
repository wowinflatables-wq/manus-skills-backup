By Sport

End-of-Season Party Ideas by Sport

Different sports have different vibes — here's what works best for each team celebration.

⚾ Baseball & Softball Teams

Baseball and softball seasons typically end in May–June, making water slides and combo units the perfect choice. Host your trophy ceremony alongside a water slide party for a memorable end-of-season bash. Works great at parks and backyards.

⚽ Soccer Teams

Soccer seasons end in fall and spring. For fall teams, bounce houses and obstacle courses are ideal. For spring teams, water slides are the crowd favorite. Our equipment fits on most soccer field sidelines and parking areas.

🏈 Football Teams

Football end-of-season parties in November–December call for dry inflatables. Obstacle courses and large bounce houses are perfect for the competitive spirit of football teams. We can set up at school grounds or parks.

🏀 Basketball Teams

Basketball seasons end in late winter or spring. Spring parties can include water slides for warmer days, while winter parties work great with dry obstacle courses and bounce houses combined with concession machines.

🏊 Swim Teams

Swim team end-of-season parties in July–August are perfect for our largest water slides and foam machines. These kids love water — give them more of it with a professional water slide setup at your pool facility or park.

🎽 Cheerleading & Dance Teams

Cheerleading and dance team end-of-season parties benefit from foam machines for a dance-party atmosphere, combined with bounce houses for the younger members. A full entertainment package makes for an unforgettable celebration.

Sports Team Party Rental FAQ

Everything coaches and parents need to know about renting inflatables for a youth sports team party in Georgia.

Can I rent a bounce house for a youth sports team end-of-season party?
Yes. Wow Inflatables provides bounce houses, water slides, obstacle courses, and concession equipment for youth sports team end-of-season parties across Middle Georgia. We deliver, set up, and pick up — perfect for baseball, soccer, football, basketball, softball, and all youth sports celebrations.
What inflatables are best for a youth sports team party?
For youth sports team parties, obstacle courses are the most popular choice — they're competitive, active, and kids love racing through them. Water slides and combo units are great for summer end-of-season parties. Concession machines like snow cone makers and popcorn machines are always a hit with kids and parents.
How much does it cost to rent inflatables for a sports team party in Georgia?
Rental prices start at $149 for a standard bounce house and $299 for obstacle courses. Water slides start at $249. Most youth sports team parties spend $300–$600 for a combination of inflatables and concessions. Call 678-379-9691 for a custom quote based on your team size and event needs.
Does Wow Inflatables deliver to parks and sports fields in Middle Georgia?
Yes. Wow Inflatables delivers to parks, sports fields, school grounds, and backyards across Middle Georgia. We provide Certificates of Insurance for parks and venues that require them. We are familiar with local park permit requirements in Macon, Warner Robins, Forsyth, Griffin, and surrounding cities.
How far in advance should I book inflatables for a sports team party?
For youth sports team parties, we recommend booking 2–3 weeks in advance. End-of-season parties often fall on the same weekends across leagues, so booking early ensures you get your preferred equipment. Call 678-379-9691 to check availability.
Can Wow Inflatables set up at a park for our team party?
Yes. We regularly set up at parks across Middle Georgia. Many parks require a permit and a Certificate of Insurance from the rental company. We provide COIs at no extra charge. Some parks also require a generator — we offer generator rentals as well. Call us to discuss your specific park location.
Do you offer discounts for youth sports team parties?
We offer competitive pricing for all events and can create custom packages for larger team celebrations. Call 678-379-9691 to discuss your team's needs and we'll put together the best package for your budget.
What if it rains on our team party day?
If weather makes it unsafe to use an inflatable, we offer free rescheduling or a full refund. We monitor weather closely and will contact you in advance if conditions are a concern. Light rain is generally fine; we cancel for lightning, high winds, or heavy rain. See our full rain policy.
