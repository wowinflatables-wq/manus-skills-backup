Frequently Asked Questions

How much space do I need for a soft play setup?
We require a flat, clear area for setup, plus an additional 5 ft clearance on all sides. We can set up indoors or outdoors on grass or hard surfaces (using sandbags).
How far in advance should I book for a 1st birthday party?
We recommend booking 3-4 weeks ahead in the summer, and 6+ weeks in advance for holiday weekends to guarantee availability of our most popular soft play sets.
What is your weather policy for outdoor soft play?
For safety, we cannot set up outdoor equipment if sustained winds exceed 15-20 mph. If rain is expected, we can often move the soft play setup indoors since it doesn't require stakes.
What happens to my deposit if it rains?
Your deposit is protected. If weather forces a cancellation, your deposit converts to a rain-check or store credit that is good for one full year.
Do you provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI)?
Yes, WOW Inflatables is fully licensed and insured. We can provide a COI for your venue, church, or community center upon request.
