Common Questions About Trunk or Treat Rentals

When should I book inflatables for a Trunk or Treat event?

You should book your Trunk or Treat rentals as early as August. October is our busiest month for church and school fall festivals, and our most popular spooky inflatables and multi-unit packages book up 6+ weeks in advance.

Do you offer multi-unit packages for large church festivals?

Yes, we offer custom quotes — free and fast — for multi-unit event packages. We can bundle bounce houses, obstacle courses, interactive games, and concession machines to accommodate hundreds of guests while fitting your organization's budget.

Do you provide a COI for school and church events?

Yes, WOW Inflatables is fully licensed and insured. We routinely provide Certificates of Insurance (COI) for schools, churches, and corporate events to meet your venue's liability requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you set up inflatables in a parking lot?
Yes, we can set up on hard surfaces like asphalt or concrete parking lots. We use heavy-duty sandbags to safely secure the units instead of stakes. Please ensure a 5 ft clearance on all sides.
How much do bounce house rentals start at?
Our standard bounce houses start at $125, combo units from $262, and obstacle courses from $272. We can build custom packages for larger events.
What happens if it rains on the day of our fall festival?
If bad weather forces a cancellation, your deposit converts to a rain-check or store credit good for one year. We cannot operate inflatables in sustained winds over 15-20 mph.
Do you provide generators for field setups?
Yes, we have generators available to rent if your event is in a field or park without access to standard power outlets within 50 feet.
Are your inflatables clean?
Absolutely. Every unit is cleaned and sanitized with a commercial-grade, eco-friendly disinfectant after every rental.
