Common Questions

Quinceañera Rental FAQ

Everything you need to know about renting inflatables for a quinceañera in Georgia.

Can I rent a bounce house for a quinceañera party in Georgia?
Yes. Wow Inflatables provides bounce houses, water slides, obstacle courses, foam machines, and concession equipment for quinceañera parties across Middle Georgia and South Metro Atlanta. We deliver, set up, and pick up — you just enjoy the celebration.
What inflatables are best for a quinceañera party?
For a quinceañera, popular choices include themed bounce houses (princess or castle styles), combo units with slides, obstacle courses for teens and adults, and foam machines for a dance-party atmosphere. Concession machines like snow cone makers and popcorn machines are also a hit. Many hosts combine 2–3 units for a full entertainment experience.
How much does it cost to rent inflatables for a quinceañera in Georgia?
Rental prices at Wow Inflatables start at $149 for a standard bounce house and go up to $799 for large combo units or obstacle courses. Packages are available for quinceañera events. Call 678-379-9691 for a custom quote based on your guest count and event needs.
Does Wow Inflatables deliver to quinceañera venues in Middle Georgia?
Yes. We provide free delivery and professional setup to backyards, event halls, parks, and venues across Middle Georgia including Macon, Warner Robins, Forsyth, Griffin, McDonough, and 40+ surrounding cities. We provide Certificates of Insurance for venues that require them.
How far in advance should I book inflatables for a quinceañera?
We recommend booking 3–6 weeks in advance for quinceañera events, especially for Saturday dates. Large quinceañera celebrations with multiple units should book 6–8 weeks ahead to ensure availability of your preferred equipment. For last-minute needs, call us at 678-379-9691 to check same-day or short-notice availability.
Can adults use the inflatables at a quinceañera?
Yes. Many of our units are designed for teens and adults. Our obstacle courses, combo units, and larger bounce houses accommodate adults. We also offer adult-specific bounce house rentals. Weight limits vary by unit — our team will help you choose the right equipment when you book.
Is Wow Inflatables licensed and insured for quinceañera events?
Yes. Wow Inflatables is fully licensed and insured in Georgia. We carry comprehensive liability insurance and can provide Certificates of Insurance for event venues at no extra charge. We are members of IAAPA (International Association of Amusement Parks and Attractions) and IOA (Inflatable Operators Association).
What is the difference between a quinceañera rental and a sweet 16 rental?
Both are milestone birthday celebrations for teenage girls — a quinceañera celebrates a girl's 15th birthday with strong Latin cultural traditions, while a sweet 16 celebrates the 16th birthday. The inflatable rental needs are similar: both benefit from obstacle courses, combo units, foam machines, and concessions for a lively teen party atmosphere. We serve both celebrations equally well.
