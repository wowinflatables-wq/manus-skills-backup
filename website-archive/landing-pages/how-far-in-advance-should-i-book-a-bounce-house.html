The Direct Answer

The Short Answer: 2–4 Weeks for Most Events

For most bounce house rentals in Middle Georgia, booking 2–4 weeks in advance is the sweet spot. This gives you enough lead time to secure your preferred unit without stressing about availability.

However, the right booking window depends on three key factors: the time of year, the day of the week, and the type of event. Here's the complete breakdown from Wow Inflatables — we've been renting bounce houses in Middle Georgia since 2005 and have seen every scenario.

Quick Reference: Booking Windows

Weekday events: 1–2 weeks ahead · Weekend events (off-peak): 2–3 weeks ahead · Weekend events (peak season): 3–4 weeks ahead · Holiday weekends: 6–8 weeks ahead · Large multi-unit events: 4–6 weeks ahead

Seasonal Guide

Booking Windows by Season in Georgia

Georgia's climate creates distinct rental seasons. Here's what to expect each time of year.

🌸

Spring (March–May)

Spring is the start of peak season in Georgia. Birthday parties, Easter celebrations, and end-of-school events fill up quickly. Book 3–4 weeks ahead for weekends. Graduation parties in May should be booked 4–6 weeks in advance — this is one of the busiest times of year.

☀️

Summer (June–August)

Peak season. Water slides and combo units are in highest demand. Holiday weekends (Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day) book out 6–8 weeks in advance. For any summer weekend, book at least 3–4 weeks ahead. Don't wait — summer is our busiest season.

🍂

Fall (September–November)

Fall is still busy with back-to-school parties, Halloween events, and church fall festivals. Labor Day weekend is extremely busy. Book 3–4 weeks ahead for September weekends, and 2–3 weeks for October–November events. More availability than summer.

❄️

Winter (December–February)

Off-peak season with the most availability. Book 1–2 weeks in advance for most winter events. Holiday parties in December can still fill up — book 3–4 weeks ahead for Christmas and New Year's events. Last-minute availability is most common in January–February.

Pro Tips

Tips for Booking Your Bounce House Rental

Book on a Weekday to Get Saturday Delivery

Many customers wait until the weekend to book, which means Saturday morning calls are common. Our team is often already on deliveries by 7 AM Saturday. Call or book online during the week to secure your spot — it takes less than 5 minutes.

Flexibility on Time Saves You Money

If you're flexible on delivery time (morning vs. afternoon), you'll have more options available. Our most popular delivery window is 8–10 AM for afternoon parties. If you can accept a 10 AM–noon delivery, you may find more availability during peak season.

What Happens If You Wait Too Long

If you wait until the week before a peak-season Saturday, your preferred unit may already be booked. You'll have three options: choose a different inflatable, change your event date, or go without. During peak season, popular water slides and obstacle courses can be booked out 4–6 weeks in advance. Booking early gives you the best selection from our 127+ unit fleet.

Last-Minute Rentals Are Sometimes Available

Don't give up if you're booking last-minute. Cancellations happen, and we sometimes have same-day or next-day availability. Call 678-379-9691 directly to check — our team can tell you immediately what's available for your date. Last-minute availability is most common on weekdays and in the off-peak months of November through February.

Large Events Need Extra Lead Time

If you're planning an event that requires multiple inflatables — a school field day, church event, HOA party, or corporate picnic — book 4–8 weeks in advance. Large events require us to reserve multiple units simultaneously, which reduces overall availability. We also need time to coordinate delivery logistics for multi-unit setups.

The Bottom Line

When in doubt, book early. You can always cancel or reschedule (subject to our cancellation policy), but you can't book a unit that's already reserved. Check availability now at wowinflatables.biz/book or call 678-379-9691.

Bounce House Booking FAQ

How far in advance should I book a bounce house rental?
In Middle Georgia, we recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance for most events. For weekend dates during peak season (April–October), book 3–4 weeks ahead. For holiday weekends like Memorial Day, 4th of July, and Labor Day, book 6–8 weeks in advance. For large events with multiple inflatables, book 4–6 weeks ahead. Last-minute bookings are sometimes available — call 678-379-9691 to check.
When is bounce house rental peak season in Georgia?
Peak season for bounce house rentals in Georgia is April through October, with the busiest months being May, June, July, and August. During these months, weekend dates fill up 3–5 weeks in advance. Off-peak months (November through March) typically have more availability, though holiday weekends still book up quickly.
Can I book a bounce house last minute in Georgia?
Yes, last-minute bounce house rentals are sometimes available in Middle Georgia. Wow Inflatables offers same-day and short-notice rentals when equipment is available. Call 678-379-9691 to check same-day or next-day availability. Last-minute availability is more common on weekdays and during off-peak months.
What happens if I wait too long to book a bounce house?
If you wait too long, your preferred unit may not be available for your date. You may need to choose a different inflatable, change your event date, or go without. During peak season weekends, popular units like large water slides and obstacle courses can be booked out 4–6 weeks in advance. Booking early gives you the best selection from our 127+ unit fleet.
Is there a deposit required to book a bounce house in Georgia?
Yes. Wow Inflatables requires a deposit to secure your booking. The deposit holds your date and equipment. The remaining balance is due before or on the day of your event. Book online or call 678-379-9691 to learn about current deposit requirements.
Can I cancel or reschedule my bounce house rental?
Yes. Wow Inflatables has a flexible cancellation and rescheduling policy. If weather makes it unsafe, we offer free rescheduling or a full refund. For customer-initiated cancellations, see our deposit and cancellation policy for details.
