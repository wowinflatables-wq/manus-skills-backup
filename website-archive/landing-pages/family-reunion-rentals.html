Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book for a summer family reunion?
Summer is peak season for reunions. We strongly advise booking 3-4 weeks ahead, and 6+ weeks in advance for major summer holiday weekends like Memorial Day or the Fourth of July.
What are the rules for setting up inflatables?
We require a flat surface (slopes under ~5% grade only) with a 5 ft clearance on all sides. Setup on grass is preferred, but we can set up on hard surfaces using sandbags. We cannot set up over septic lines.
Are you insured to operate in county parks?
Yes, WOW Inflatables is fully licensed and insured. We can provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) naming the specific city or county park as additionally insured if required by your pavilion permit.
What is your cancellation policy if it rains?
If weather forces a cancellation (such as sustained winds over 15-20 mph), your deposit converts to a rain-check or store credit that is good for one full year. You won't lose your money.
Do you clean the equipment before delivery?
Yes, every unit is cleaned and sanitized with a commercial-grade, eco-friendly disinfectant after every rental.
