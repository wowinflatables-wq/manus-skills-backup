Frequently Asked Questions

Are your inflatables inspected for safety?
Yes, all our inflatables undergo regular safety inspections and are set up by our trained professionals. We require a 5 ft clearance on all sides and ensure the units are properly anchored.
How do you sanitize the equipment between rentals?
Every unit is cleaned and sanitized with a commercial-grade, eco-friendly disinfectant after every rental. We know how important hygiene is for preschool environments.
Can you set up a bounce house in our daycare parking lot?
Yes, we can set up on hard surfaces like a parking lot or playground using heavy-duty sandbags instead of stakes. We prefer grass, but can safely accommodate asphalt.
What is the weather policy for our scheduled water day?
If sustained winds exceed 15-20 mph or severe weather hits, we cannot safely operate the inflatables. If we must cancel due to weather, your deposit converts to a rain-check or store credit good for one year.
How far in advance should a daycare book for end-of-year parties?
End-of-year parties in May and summer camp kickoffs in June are our busiest times. We highly recommend booking 3-4 weeks ahead to ensure you get the specific toddler units you want.
