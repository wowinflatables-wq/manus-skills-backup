## Product / Rental Categories

### 1. Inflatable Bounce Houses
> Bounce Houses for Every Occasion. We stock a wide variety of inflatable rentals to fit any space or budget. Our inventory includes: Classic Bouncers (open play areas perfect for younger jumpers), Combos & Slides (featuring climbing walls, slides, and hoops), and Themed Units (colorful designs to match your party theme). All rentals are professionally cleaned and ideal for private parties or large community events.

**Starting price:** From $140

### 2. Bounce & Slide Combos
> The Ultimate Party Upgrade. Take your event to the next level with our most popular inventory category. Perfect for larger crowds, these dynamic inflatables merge the classic fun of a jumper with obstacle course elements to keep the action moving — whether it's a backyard birthday or a large community festival.

### 3. Water Slides & Dry Slides
> Wet or Dry: Slides for Any Season. Choose from dual-lane water slides with pools for competitive racing, or towering dry slides perfect for fall carnivals. We stock everything from compact units for small yards to complex "water park" style attractions.

**Starting price:** From $170

### 4. Interactive Games
> More Than Just Inflatables. Ignite the spirit of competition at your next Middle Georgia gathering. We offer dozens of interactive challenges designed to get people off the sidelines — from head-to-head obstacle races to strategic challenges that test skill and speed. Perfect for church events and company picnics.

**Starting price:** From $99

### 5. Concessions
> Treats for Every Taste. Beat the Middle Georgia heat with a refreshing snow cone, or satisfy a sweet tooth with pink vanilla cotton candy. Machines and supplies sold separately.

### 6. Amusements
> From a Mechanical Bear to a Rock Climbing Wall, and from the Wizzer to our maze, you will love these show stoppers. Amusements include carnival games, face painting, balloon animals, and mechanical rides.
