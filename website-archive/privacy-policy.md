# Privacy Policy — Wow Inflatables

*Effective Date: February 22, 2026*
*Archived from www.wowjumpparty.com — July 2026*

---

At Wow Inflatables, we take your privacy as seriously as we take your safety. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you book a rental, visit our website, or sign up for our communications.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information that helps us deliver, set up, and manage your inflatable rentals:

- **Contact Information:** Name, email address, phone number, and physical billing/delivery addresses.
- **Event Details:** Date of event, type of celebration, and specific setup instructions (e.g., "gate code" or "setup on grass").
- **Payment Data:** Credit card numbers or other payment details are processed through secure, PCI-compliant third-party processors. We do not store full credit card numbers on our local servers.
- **Usage Data:** Information about how you use our website, including IP addresses and cookies, to improve your browsing experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to ensure your event is a success:

- **Service Delivery:** To process bookings, send receipts, and coordinate delivery/pickup of bounce houses and equipment.
- **Communication:** To send transactional updates (e.g., weather alerts affecting your rental) and marketing materials if you have opted in.
- **Safety & Logistics:** To ensure equipment is delivered to the correct location and set up according to safety standards.

## 3. Messaging & Marketing Consent

If you provide your mobile number or email:

- **SMS/Email:** You may receive automated messages regarding your order or promotional offers.
- **No Sharing:** We do not sell, rent, or share your opt-in consent or personal data with third parties for their independent marketing purposes.
- **Opt-Out:** You can unsubscribe from emails via the "Unsubscribe" link or stop text messages by replying STOP at any time.

## 4. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal or tax obligations. We implement industry-standard security measures (SSL encryption, secure firewalls) to protect your data from unauthorized access.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request the correction or deletion of your data.
- Withdraw your consent for marketing at any time.

## 6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (like Google Analytics or rental management software) to help run our business. These partners only have access to the information needed to perform their specific tasks and are obligated to protect your privacy.
