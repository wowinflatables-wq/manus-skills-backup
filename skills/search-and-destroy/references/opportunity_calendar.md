# Regional Opportunity Calendar & Search Patterns

This reference expands the core opportunity calendar for the **Southeast US / Middle Georgia** climate (where outdoor season runs March through November) and provides exact search query patterns to find live events.

## Month-by-Month Opportunities

### Spring (March – May)
**Easter & Spring Festivals**
- **Window:** 30-45 days before Easter (March/April)
- **Targets:** Churches, city parks & rec, HOAs
- **Search Patterns:** 
  - `"[town]" "Easter egg hunt" [year]`
  - `"[county] GA" "spring festival" OR "spring fling"`
  - `"[town]" church "Easter festival"`
- **Best Inventory:** Standard bounce houses, toddler units, obstacle courses.

**School Field Days & End-of-Year Carnivals**
- **Window:** Pitch in April for May events
- **Targets:** Elementary and middle schools, PTAs/PTOs
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" elementary "field day" [year]`
  - `"[county] GA" school "spring carnival" OR "end of year bash"`
  - `site:facebook.com "[town] PTO" OR "[town] PTA"`
- **Best Inventory:** Obstacle courses, large slides, interactive games (joust, wrecking ball), snow cone machines.

### Summer (June – August)
**Vacation Bible School (VBS)**
- **Window:** Pitch in May/June for June/July events
- **Targets:** Local churches (Baptist, Methodist, non-denominational are largest)
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"vacation bible school" OR VBS "[town]" [year]`
  - `"[town]" church VBS registration [year]`
- **Best Inventory:** Bounce houses, combos, water slides (if outdoor), obstacle courses.

**Summer Camps & Daycares**
- **Window:** Pitch in May for June-July recurring rentals
- **Targets:** YMCA, local day camps, martial arts summer camps, large daycares
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "summer camp" registration [year]`
  - `"[county] GA" "day camp" OR "summer program"`
- **Best Inventory:** Water slides, combos (often rented on a weekly rotation).

**July 4th & Summer Community Events**
- **Window:** Pitch in late May/June for early July events
- **Targets:** City governments, large HOAs, country clubs
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "July 4th celebration" OR "Independence Day"`
  - `"[town]" HOA "summer kickoff" OR "pool party"`
- **Best Inventory:** Large water slides, slip-n-slides, dunk tanks, cotton candy machines.

**Back-to-School Bashes**
- **Window:** Pitch in July for late July/August events
- **Targets:** Churches, apartment complexes, schools
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "back to school bash" OR "kickoff"`
  - `"[county]" church "back to school"`
- **Best Inventory:** Combos, water slides (still hot in GA), obstacle courses.

### Fall (September – November)
**Fall Festivals & Harvest Fests**
- **Window:** Pitch in August/September for October events
- **Targets:** Churches, elementary schools, local farms
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "fall festival" OR "harvest festival" [year]`
  - `"[county] GA" church "fall fest"`
- **Best Inventory:** Large dry slides, combos, obstacle courses, interactive games, popcorn machines.

**Trunk-or-Treat / Halloween Events**
- **Window:** Pitch in September for late October events
- **Targets:** Churches, schools, community centers
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "trunk or treat" [year]`
  - `"[county] GA" "Halloween festival" OR "fall carnival"`
- **Best Inventory:** Bounce houses, combos, obstacle courses (huge demand, book early).

### Winter (December – February)
**Holiday Events & Winter Festivals**
- **Window:** Pitch in November for December events
- **Targets:** Cities, malls, large churches
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" "Christmas festival" OR "winter wonderland"`
  - `"[town]" "breakfast with Santa"`
- **Best Inventory:** Indoor-compatible units (under 15ft tall), photo backdrops, concessions.

**Lock-ins & Indoor Winter Events**
- **Window:** Pitch in December/January for Jan/Feb events
- **Targets:** Church youth groups, school lock-ins
- **Search Patterns:**
  - `"[town]" youth "lock-in" OR "winter retreat"`
  - `"[county] GA" "daddy daughter dance"`
- **Best Inventory:** Indoor obstacle courses, interactive games (joust, bungee run).

## Qualification Criteria for Middle Georgia

When evaluating leads for Wow Inflatables:
1. **Distance:** Must be within 30 miles of Forsyth, GA 31029 (covers Macon, Jackson, Thomaston, etc.).
2. **Timing:** Event date must be 30-60 days out (the ideal booking window).
3. **Public Contact:** Must find a public email or contact form for the organization. Do not use personal/private emails.
4. **Fit:** The event type must match the available inventory (e.g., don't pitch water slides for indoor winter events).
