# Theme Library for Package Prize

This library provides expanded theme and color-palette ideas for various event types. Use these as a starting point when designing a package or its accompanying ad.

## Event Types & Themes

### Birthday Parties
*   **Theme:** Princess / Fairytale
    *   **Color Palette:** Pink (#FFC0CB), Gold (#FFD700), White (#FFFFFF)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Castles, tiaras, wands, glitter, carriages.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Magical, royal, enchanting.
*   **Theme:** Superhero
    *   **Color Palette:** Red (#FF0000), Blue (#0000FF), Yellow (#FFFF00)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Comic book action bubbles (BAM!, POW!), capes, city skylines.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Action-packed, energetic, bold.
*   **Theme:** Safari / Jungle
    *   **Color Palette:** Green (#228B22), Brown (#8B4513), Khaki (#F0E68C)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Animal prints, vines, safari hats, binoculars.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Adventurous, wild, exploratory.

### Seasonal & Holiday Events
*   **Theme:** Trunk-or-Treat / Fall Festival
    *   **Color Palette:** Orange (#FFA500), Black (#000000), Purple (#800080)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Pumpkins, hay bales, autumn leaves, friendly ghosts.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Festive, spooky-but-friendly, community-focused.
*   **Theme:** Winter Wonderland
    *   **Color Palette:** Ice Blue (#ADD8E6), Silver (#C0C0C0), White (#FFFFFF)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Snowflakes, icicles, snowmen, pinecones.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Crisp, magical, festive.

### Community & School Events
*   **Theme:** VBS (Vacation Bible School) / Church Festival
    *   **Color Palette:** Varies by annual theme (often bright, engaging colors like Teal #008080 and Yellow #FFFF00)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Depends on the curriculum (e.g., space, ocean, desert).
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Welcoming, educational, fun.
*   **Theme:** Graduation
    *   **Color Palette:** School Colors (e.g., Black #000000 & Gold #FFD700)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Diplomas, graduation caps, "Class of [Year]" banners.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Celebratory, proud, momentous.

### Specialty & Corporate
*   **Theme:** Luau / Tropical
    *   **Color Palette:** Hot Pink (#FF69B4), Lime Green (#32CD32), Ocean Blue (#00BFFF)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Tiki torches, leis, palm leaves, pineapples.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Relaxed, summery, festive.
*   **Theme:** Glow / Neon
    *   **Color Palette:** Neon Pink (#FF10F0), Neon Green (#39FF14), Black (#000000)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Glow sticks, blacklights, neon paint splatters.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** High-energy, modern, nightlife.
*   **Theme:** Candy / Sweets
    *   **Color Palette:** Pastel Pink (#FFD1DC), Mint Green (#98FF98), Lavender (#E6E6FA)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Lollipops, gumdrops, sprinkles, cotton candy.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Sweet, playful, whimsical.
*   **Theme:** Sports
    *   **Color Palette:** Varies by sport (e.g., Football: Brown #8B4513 & Green #228B22)
    *   **Décor Elements:** Balls, goals, whistles, jerseys.
    *   **Mood/Vibe:** Competitive, active, team-oriented.
